Projects

Parking Space Reservation Management System

A web-based Parking Space Reservation Management System developed to simplify and automate the parking process by allowing users to reserve parking slots in advance through an online platform. The system provides real-time information about parking slot availability, enabling drivers to quickly find and book suitable parking spaces without wasting time searching manually. This application includes both user and admin modules. Users can register, log in, view available parking slots, reserve a slot, and receive booking confirmation. The admin can manage parking slots, monitor vehicle entries and exits, generate reports, and maintain parking records. The system also helps in reducing traffic congestion caused by vehicles searching for parking spaces and improves the overall parking management efficiency.
